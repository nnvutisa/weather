Monthly Average Percipitation
 

The monthly average precipitation data is obtained from the NOAA 1981-2010 climate normal dataset. The dataset provides 30-year averages for each month of precipitation values measured at U.S. climate and weather stations. The state values are unweighted averages from all the stations within the state.
